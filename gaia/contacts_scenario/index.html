FAQ

Q: What plays will be posted?

A: All games scored on this device are posted automatically.

A: Any games in your History are posted when you click the Post to BGG button.

Q: Can someone else post for me?

A: Only the account entered above will have the game posted. You can change the login and repost from the History section. Alternatively, if you share a cloud account you can post from an account on a different device from the history section once the session has synced.

Q: Is my password secure?

A: Yes. First it is sent encrypted over HTTPS to our secure server, which posts it to BoardGameGeek.com just like if you were signing in on their website. BGG authenticates your login and sends our server back a temporary login token. From that point the app uses the token instead of your password for all subsequent non-secure interactions over standard HTTP with BGG.

Q: Is my password saved in the cloud?

A: No, your password is only saved on this device.

FAQ

Q: What data is pushed to the cloud?

A: Players, Games, Sessions, Scores, and Achievements. Sensitive data like your BGG password, and large files like photos, are never pushed to the cloud.

Q: Can I share my login?

A: Yes, it is designed to be shared between multiple users to sync all your data between many devices.

Q: How many accounts can I create?

A: As many as you like, but data will only be posted to the account you are currently logged into. Any time you log into an account it is synced with this device.

Q: Do I have to pay for storage?

A: No, it's 100% free.

Q: Do you require personal information?

A: No, but you might want to provide your email address for password retrieval, we won't spam or divulge it.

Q: Whose data will be pushed to my device?

A: Only other players logging in to the cloud with the same username and password.

Q: Can I remove players someone else added?

A: Deleting them will remove them from all devices. You can prevent a player from being displayed on a specific device from the Edit Players section.
